Text commands! Nothing more, nothing less.

---

- **`69emojify`** - Make the bot say whatever you want with emojis!

  - **Usage**: `69emojify <text/message>`
  - **Cooldown**: `10s`

---

- **`69gooba`** - Sends gooba lyrics.

  - **Usage**: `69gooba`
  - **Cooldown**: `3s`

---

- **`69swear`** - Random entities do be swearing doe

  - **Usage**: `69swear`
  - **Cooldown**: `3s`

---
